Ganta RamuluYadhav

GantaRamulu(born 12 May 1976), frequently known by his initials Ramanna, is an Indian politician currently representing the peddapali constituency and serving as the leader of the opposition in the Telangana Legislative Zila Parshad.

Earliylife and Education

GantaRamulu was born to GantaRajaiah and late GantaKomuramma on 12 May 1976 in Haripuram village, peddapali ,State ( Telangana). He is of the BC-Yadhav caste. Ramulu has one sister and three brothers. He Completed Inter in Arts collage and Degree in open University, Karimnager

PersonlLife

Ramulu is married to Akshitha and has two children.His son name is Ganta AdvaitRam and His daughter name is vighanathri. Ramulu is proficient in Telugu, English and Hindi. He lives with his family at Bankcolony ,Karimnagar.
